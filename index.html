Welcome to the Familiarity Form!

Thank you for taking the time to fill out this survey.
This should take no more than 5 minutes to complete.

In this survey, you will see faces of various people.
We want to know if you recognize these people or not.
In other words, we want to know if the person is VISUALLY familiar to you.

For example, even if you do not know a person that well,
if you recognize their face and know you have seen them before,
please mark that person as familiar.

If you do not recognize the person,
and do not think you have ever seen them before,
you can mark them as unfamiliar.
